About This Role

The team already runs another YouTube channel. This is their second one. The scripts are ready. The style is set. Your job is to edit the videos to match the style - not figure it out from scratch.

We start with 10 videos, then 2 every week. As the channel grows, so does the workload. You get paid per video when you deliver. Do good work and you stay. This is steady, long-term income.

What You Will Be Editing

Long YouTube videos, 10 to 20 minutes each. Fun and high-energy. Not boring. Not corporate.

  • Two video files per video - one of the presenter talking to camera, one of the computer screen with voiceover. You combine them. The idea is always explained first, then shown on screen.
  • Screen recordings - the screen fills the full frame. The presenter's face shows in a small window in the corner (Picture-in-Picture).
  • Animated captions - words appear one by one as the presenter speaks. Key words are highlighted in colour. No plain white text at the bottom.
  • Memes and sound effects - clips from TV, movies, the internet. This is part of the style.
  • Text on screen - for key points and numbers
  • Diagrams on screen - step-by-step visuals, numbered lists, concept breakdowns
