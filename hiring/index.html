What You'll Be Editing

Long YouTube videos, 10-20 minutes each. The videos teach small business owners about AI - but in a fun, high-energy way. Not boring. Not corporate.

  • Two-pass footage - we film the presenter talking to camera, then film the screen with voiceover. You cut and match them. Rule: explain the idea first, then show it on screen.

  • Memes and sound effects - clips from TV, movies, the internet. This is part of the style, not optional.
  • Text on screen - for key points and numbers
  • Diagrams and visuals - step-by-step breakdowns drawn or animated on screen
