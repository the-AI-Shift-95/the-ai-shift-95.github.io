What You'll Be Editing

Long-form YouTube videos (10-20 min). The style is edutainment - educational content with real entertainment energy. Not corporate explainer videos.

  • Split capture stitching - raw footage comes in two passes: face-to-camera and screenshare with voiceover. Cut and match them cleanly. Structure is always: explain the concept first, then show it.
  • Screen recording walkthroughs - screenshare as main view, presenter face in a small Picture in Picture window in the corner
  • Animated captions - word-by-word reveal style, key phrases colour-highlighted. Not static text at the bottom.
  • Meme inserts and sound effects - internet humour, TV/movie clips, audio references. This is not optional.
  • Text overlays for key points and stats
  • Concept explanation sequences - diagrams, frameworks, data breakdowns drawn or animated on screen
