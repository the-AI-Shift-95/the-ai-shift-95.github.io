The CTA — Viewer Deliverable Strategy

What you offer at the end of the video. How it gets delivered. Why this approach converts.

The Problem With Standard Lead Magnets
Most YouTube CTAs — "download the free template" — produce a list of cold emails and a Google Doc nobody opens. The file isn't useful because it isn't personal. A generic AI Co-CEO template tells Sai's viewers nothing about their targets, their team, or their tools. They download it, open it once, and forget it. The channel gets email addresses. The viewer gets nothing that changes their behavior. That's a bad trade for both sides.
The Approach: Two-Stage Delivery

Email captured first. Personalisation happens after — inside the AI tool they're already about to use. Here's the exact viewer journey:

1
CTA in video: single-line ask
"Link in the description — grab your free AI Co-CEO Starter File. Takes 30 seconds."
One sentence. One action. No multi-part instructions on camera.
2
Landing page: email only
One field. One button. "Get your free AI Co-CEO Starter File." Email captured here — nothing else. If they close the tab after this step, the lead is still ours.
Why email first: data shows 40-55% drop-off on multi-question forms. Capture the lead before asking anything else.
3
Thank-you page: 3 quick questions (optional upside)
"While your file is on its way — 3 quick questions so we can personalise it." All multiple choice, zero open text. Example: "What's your biggest time drain?" / "Which tools do you run your business on?" / "What's your team size?"
The starter file is already sending regardless. Questions are bonus input — for them, a better file. For us, richer data for future videos.
4
Email delivery: the Starter File arrives within 60 seconds
Subject: "Your AI Co-CEO Starter File is here." Body: short. File attached as ai-ceo-starter-file.md. Below the file: three-paragraph instructions — one per platform.
Claude: Open claude.ai → New Project → paste the file contents into Project Instructions. Then ask: "Interview me to complete my AI Co-CEO setup."
ChatGPT: Settings → Personalisation → Custom Instructions → paste the file contents. Or open a new GPT and paste it as the system prompt.
Gemini: Open a new Gem → paste contents into system instructions. Or paste the file at the top of any conversation.
5
Stage 2: the AI completes the personalisation
The Starter File has a prompt baked in at the top: "You are setting up your AI Co-CEO. I'm going to ask you 8 questions about your business — your targets, your team, your tools. Answer them and I'll build your complete context file."
The AI interviews them. The output is genuinely personalised — because it adapted to their exact answers in real time, not a mail-merge swap. This is what a static template can never do.
Why the two-stage model wins
Stage 1 (email + form) handles reach — it converts as many people as possible. Stage 2 (AI interview) handles depth — it creates something so specific to their business they actually use it. These are different jobs. Don't ask one mechanism to do both.
What Needs to Be Built (Apurv Owns)
  • Landing page: Single-field email capture. Hosted on GitHub Pages or Netlify. Matches brand system.
  • 3-question form: Typeform or Tally (free tier). All multiple choice. Connected to Zapier.
  • Zapier workflow: Form submission → fill .md template with answers → email via Gmail/SendGrid with file attached.
  • The Starter File template: Pre-written .md with 8-question AI interview prompt at the top + placeholder sections for their business context. Partially filled from form answers if available, otherwise blank with clear instructions.
  • Email template: Short, warm, actionable. Platform instructions inline — not in a separate doc. Attachment: ai-ceo-starter-file.md.
Decision Confirmed
This is the CTA approach for the AI CEO video. The Starter File replaces the previous "Claude interview script" concept. It works across all AI platforms. It doesn't require the viewer to have Claude. The build is straightforward — no custom backend, no API, just Typeform + Zapier + a well-written .md file.
